## Enterprise PaaS, Without Compromise

Unlike generic container platforms or developer tools, **codbex** is designed as a complete application platform:

- **Application development and runtime** (Java, JavaScript, APIs, events)
- **Workflow and process automation** (BPMN-based, enterprise-ready)
- **Integrated data services** and analytics
- **Built-in security**, identity, and lifecycle management
- **Multi-tenant** and self-service capabilities for internal teams

All of this runs on sovereign European IaaS - such as EU-based cloud providers and on-premise infrastructure - ensuring compliance with GDPR, data residency requirements, and sector-specific regulations.
